Who is Yong Yang?

Yong Yang is a student at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. His research interests broadly include HPC(High Performance Computing) and wireless communications, and more specifically focus on using FPGAs to accelerate HPC. In other words, he thinks in depth about some numerical calculation problems. He's fond of using computational techniques to attack problems in wireless communications.

Prior to studying for a master's degree, he received an undergraduate degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.
